# Tenta Sorella Brella

The Tenta Sorella Brella is a main weapon in Splatoon 2 and Splatoon 3 . It is a Sorella -branded version of the Tenta Brella and comes with a different sub weapon and special weapon .

- **Class**: Brella
- **Sub**: Ink Mine
- **Special**: Trizooka
- **Special points**: 200p
- **Range**: 62/100
- **Damage**: 85/100
- **Durability**: 85/100
- **Level**: 22
- **Added in**: Sizzle Season 2023 ( 4.0.0 )
- **Other variants**: Tenta Brella Tenta Brella CRE-M

## Appearance

The Tenta Sorella Brella is nearly identical in appearance to the Tenta Brella . The only difference between the two is that the Tenta Sorella Brella has two white Inkline logos on the color-changing parts of the canopy facing the muzzle. In Splatoon 3 , the rails supporting the canopy are also yellow instead of red.

## Trivia

Unlike the other secondary variants of brellas , the Tenta Sorella Brella does not have a unique pattern on its canopy. However, in Splatoon 3 , the support beams are yellow instead of red.
