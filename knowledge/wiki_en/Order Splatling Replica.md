# Order Splatling Replica

The Order Splatling Replica is a main weapon in Splatoon 3 . It is a reskin of the Heavy Splatling , and a direct successor to the Splatoon 2 Hero Splatling Replica .

## Splatoon 3

- **Class**: Splatling
- **Sub**: Sprinkler
- **Special**: Wave Breaker
- **Special points**: 210p
- **Range**: 78/100
- **Charge speed**: 38/100
- **Mobility**: 55/100
- **Level**: 0
- **Added in**: Side Order ( 7.0.0 )
- **Base damage**: 30
- **Other variants**: Heavy Splatling Heavy Splatling Deco

## Appearance

The Order Splatling Replica is designed to look like a simplified version of the Heavy Splatling , containing only white and gray colors.
