# Order Stringer

The Order Stringer is a stringer in Splatoon 3 that is exclusively used in Side Order .

## Appearance

The Order Stringer is based on the Tri-Stringer , but has a minimalist appearance, with a single white molded piece for the bow and tubes of the weapon, as well as a metallic silver ink tank pump, reel, and nozzle. When firing, ring-shaped holograms manifest around the nozzle.

## Side Order

The Order Stringer is a weapon unlocked in Side Order . Its base set consists of Toxic Mist and Killer Wail 5.1 , notably identical to that of the Tri-Stringer . However, this can be changed in the Spire of Order by using a vending machine .

It has lower stats in comparison to the Tri-Stringer, with a slower fire rate, explosion damage, strafe speed, ink efficiency, range, charge time, and is unable to store its charge, but has higher projectile damage. However, this can be upgraded through color chips and Marina's Hacks .

Shiver's Palette is one of the twelve Palettes in Side Order . It can be found in one of the lockers in front of the Spire of Order and is set to the Order Stringer. Defeating Overlorder with this Palette for the first time unlocks the Order Stringer Replica for use in online battles.
