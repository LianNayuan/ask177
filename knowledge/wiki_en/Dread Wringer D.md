# Dread Wringer D

The Dread Wringer D is a main weapon in Splatoon 3 . It is the "D" brand version of the Dread Wringer .

## Splatoon 3

- **Class**: Slosher
- **Sub**: Squid Beakon
- **Special**: Wave Breaker
- **Special points**: 190p
- **Range**: 60/100
- **Damage**: 55/100
- **Handling**: 35/100
- **Level**: 18
- **Added in**: Fresh Season 2024
- **Other variants**: Dread Wringer Hornz Dread Wringer

## Appearance

The Dread Wringer D appears broadly similar to the base Dread Wringer, but its lower bucket is black instead of light gray, its metal frame is a tarnished speckled brown, and it has a "D" brand sticker on each side.

## Etymology

"D" is from the manufacturer of this weapon, the " Dolphin " brand.
