# Order Dualie Replicas

The Order Dualie Replicas are a main weapon in Splatoon 3 . They are a variant of the Splat Dualies with the same stats, but with the visual design of the Order Dualies . They are a direct successor to the Splatoon 2 Hero Dualie Replicas .

## Splatoon 3

- **Class**: Dualie
- **Sub**: Suction Bomb
- **Special**: Crab Tank
- **Special points**: 200p
- **Range**: 50/100
- **Damage**: 29/100
- **Mobility**: 60/100
- **Level**: 0
- **Added in**: 22 February 2024 ( Side Order ) ( 7.0.0 )
- **Base damage**: 30
- **Other variants**: Splat Dualies Enperry Splat Dualies Twinklez Splat Dualies

## Appearance

The Order Dualie Replicas are identical in appearance to the Order Dualies . They are mainly white with silver nozzles.
