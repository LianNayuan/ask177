# Z+F Splat Charger

It is a rebranded successor of Firefin Splat Charger from Splatoon 2 following the merger between Firefin and Zekko to form Z+F.

## Splatoon 3

- **Class**: Charger
- **Sub**: Splash Wall
- **Special**: Triple Inkstrike
- **Special points**: 200p
- **Range**: 88/100
- **Charge speed**: 50/100
- **Mobility**: 40/100
- **Level**: 9
- **Added in**: Fresh Season 2023 ( 3.0.0 )
- **Other variants**: Splat Charger Splat Charger CAM-O Order Charger Replica

## Appearance

The Z+F Splat Charger shares its overall design with the Splat Charger . However, the purple on the base is a lighter shade matching the Z+F logo, the ink tank shares that color and does not change color with ink , the ink tank has the written version of the Z+F logo on it, the ink tank has a number of icons of ZedFry on it, all yellow parts are instead orange, the blue sections are a lighter shade of blue, and a sticker of a hollow star matching the lighter shade of blue is visible on either side of of the barrel near the sights that are closer to the user.
