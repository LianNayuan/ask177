# REEF-LUX 450 MIL-K

The REEF-LUX 450 MIL-K is a main weapon in the Splatoon series . It is a Barazushi -branded variant of the REEF-LUX 450 from the Splatlands Collection and features a different sub and special weapon .

## Splatoon 3

- **Class**: Stringer
- **Sub**: Torpedo
- **Special**: Booyah Bomb
- **Special points**: 210p
- **Range**: 60/100
- **Charge speed**: 75/100
- **Mobility**: 60/100
- **Level**: 16
- **Added in**: Splatlands Collection ( 10.0.0 )
- **Other variants**: REEF-LUX 450 REEF-LUX 450 Deco

## Appearance

The REEF-LUX 450 MIL-K is an alteration of the REEF-LUX 450 . The central black part is changed to white, and its plastic is now slightly translucent, which makes the color of the ink-colored bottle inside visible from certain angles. The strings are orange instead of white, along with the wheels being octagonal instead of circular. Additionally, a squid-shaped carabiner is attached to the top of the weapon.

## Trivia

The REEF-LUX 450 MIL-K uses a slightly lower quality model than its counterparts. This is most obvious on the wheels on each end of the weapon, which have less polygons than the other two.

## Etymology

The suffix "MIL-K" is a stylized spelling of the word milk .
