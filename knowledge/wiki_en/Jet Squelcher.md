## Appearance

The Jet Squelcher has two main parts, both blue. The handhold housing the trigger is in between them. The top part is home to multiple warning labels and a black knob. Sticking out of the top part is a long black barrel with a dull white nozzle at the end. A black tube connects the two parts.

## Trivia

The design of the Jet Squelcher appears to be based on a Pressure Washer .

## Etymology

Jet Squelcher is derived from "Jet Washer", a name pressure washers are commonly marketed under with Squelcher meaning "to squelch", being an onomatopoeia of splashing liquid.
