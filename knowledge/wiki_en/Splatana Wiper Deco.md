# Splatana Wiper Deco

The Splatana Wiper Deco is a main weapon in Splatoon 3 . It is the Deco -branded version of the Splatana Wiper , with identical stats.

## Splatoon 3

- **Class**: Splatana
- **Sub**: Squid Beakon
- **Special**: Tenta Missiles
- **Special points**: 190p
- **Range**: 58/100
- **Damage**: 29/100
- **Handling**: 75/100
- **Level**: 10
- **Added in**: Sizzle Season 2023 ( 4.0.0 )
- **Base damage**: 30 (Projectile-tap) 15 (Direct-tap) 60 (Projectile-charge) 120 (Direct-charge) 50 ( SRNW Projectile-tap) 35 ( SRNW Direct-tap) 150 ( SRNW Projectile-charge) 175 ( SRNW Direct-charge)
- **Ink consumption**: 3% (Tap) 6% (Hold)
- **Other variants**: Splatana Wiper Splatana Wiper RUS-T

## Appearance

The Splatana Wiper Deco remains physically similar to the original variant, but with a white-gray color scheme instead of the usual black, with Deco chevron stickers on either side of the wiper blade, and chevron charms on the handguard.

## Trivia

The Splatana Wiper Deco is the first weapon post-launch to have Tenta Missiles .

The Splatana Wiper Deco shares the same sub and special as the Neo Sploosh-o-matic and the Octobrush Nouveau had in Splatoon 2 .
