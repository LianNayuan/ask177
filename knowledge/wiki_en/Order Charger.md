# Order Charger

The Order Charger is a charger in Splatoon 3 that is exclusively used in Side Order .

## Appearance

The Order Charger is based on the Splat Charger , but has a minimalist appearance, with a single white molded piece for the barrel and handle of the weapon, as well as a metallic silver ink tank and nozzle. When firing, ring-shaped holograms manifest around the nozzle and base of the Charger.

## Side Order

The Order Charger is a weapon unlocked in Side Order . Its base set consists of Ink Mines and Triple Inkstrike . However, this can be changed in the Spire of Order by using a vending machine .

It has lower stats in comparison to the Splat Charger , with a slower fire rate, strafe speed, ink efficiency, charge time, range, and is unable to store its charge. However, this can be upgraded through color chips and Marina's Hacks .

Marie's Palette is one of the twelve Palettes in Side Order . It can be found in one of the lockers in front of the Spire of Order and is set to the Order Charger. Defeating Overlorder with this Palette for the first time unlocks the Order Charger Replica for use in online battles.
