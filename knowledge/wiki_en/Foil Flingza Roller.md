# Foil Flingza Roller

The Foil Flingza Roller is a main weapon in Splatoon 2 and Splatoon 3 . It is a Foil -branded version of the Flingza Roller , and it comes with a different loadout.

## Appearance

In Splatoon 2 , the Foil Flingza Roller is nearly identical in appearance to the Flingza Roller . The only difference between the two is that the Foil Flingza Roller has a sticker bearing the Foil logo on the Swiss Army Knife .

In Splatoon 3 , the Foil sticker is still present, but the weapon's color has changed from red to blue.

## Trivia

In Splatoon 2 , its icon has the triangular version of the Foil icon, whereas in Splatoon 3 , it shares that from the Foil Squeezer . In both games, the weapon itself uses the triangle sticker.
