# Light Tetra Dualies

The Light Tetra Dualies are a main weapon in Splatoon 2 and Splatoon 3 . They are a variant of the Dark Tetra Dualies with a different look and loadout.

## Splatoon 3

- **Class**: Dualie
- **Sub**: Sprinkler
- **Special**: Zipcaster
- **Special points**: 190p
- **Range**: 58/100
- **Damage**: 22/100
- **Mobility**: 90/100
- **Level**: 24
- **Added in**: Sizzle Season 2023 ( 4.0.0 )
- **Other variant**: Dark Tetra Dualies

## Splatoon 2

- **Class**: Dualie
- **Sub**: Sprinkler
- **Special**: Autobomb Launcher
- **Special points**: 200p
- **Range**: 58/100
- **Damage**: 22/100
- **Mobility**: 90/100
- **Level**: 21
- **Cost**: 13,300
- **Added in**: 1 August 2018
- **Base damage**: 28
- **Ink consumption**: 0.8% 3% (Roll)
- **Other variant**: Dark Tetra Dualies

## Appearance

The Light Tetra Dualies are similar in appearance to the Dark Tetra Dualies . However, the black part of the Light Tetra Dualies is white, and the front part has scarlet-colored highlights instead of purple and green.

## Trivia

The Light Tetra Dualies were shown among the new items in Splatoon 2 version 4.0.0 .
