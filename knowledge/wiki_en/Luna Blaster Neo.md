## Appearance

In Splatoon and Splatoon 2 , the Luna Blaster Neo is nearly identical in appearance to the Luna Blaster , with the only difference being a sticker bearing the Neo icon on the front half of the blaster in the former and the Neo icon and logo written in Inkling embossed on the front half of the blaster in the latter.

In Splatoon 3 , the Luna Blaster Neo's body is black instead of its base counterpart's white.
