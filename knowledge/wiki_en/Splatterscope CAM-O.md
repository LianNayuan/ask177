# Splatterscope CAM-O

The Splatterscope CAM-O is a main weapon in the Splatoon series . It is a Barazushi -branded variant of the the Splatterscope from the Splatlands Collection and features a different sub and special weapon .

## Splatoon 3

- **Class**: Charger
- **Sub**: Sprinkler
- **Special**: Crab Tank
- **Special points**: 200p
- **Range**: 91/100
- **Charge speed**: 50/100
- **Mobility**: 30/100
- **Level**: 17
- **Added in**: Splatlands Collection ( 10.0.0 )
- **Other variants**: Splatterscope Z+F Splatterscope

## Appearance

The Splatterscope CAM-O is nearly identical to the Splat Charger CAM-O . The only differences are that the sights that are closer to the user are removed, with the sights at the end of the barrel being maintained, and it has an added scope. The scope is almost identical to the scope on the Splatterscope , but the parts that change color with ink color are instead a reflective chrome regardless of ink color.

## Trivia

The original tweet announcing this weapon accidentally used the Japanese suffix "FRST" to refer to Splat Charger CAM-O instead of the localized "CAM-O".

The Splatterscope CAM-O's appearance could be a reference to the Kelp Splatterscope , given the kelp stuck to the side of the ink tank.

## Etymology

The brand stylization suffix "CAM-O" could be interpreted as camo or camouflage .
