# Clash Blaster Neo

The Clash Blaster Neo is a main weapon in Splatoon 2 and Splatoon 3 . It is a Neo -branded version of the Clash Blaster and comes with a different sub weapon and special weapon .

## Splatoon 3

- **Class**: Blaster
- **Sub**: Curling Bomb
- **Special**: Super Chump
- **Special points**: 170p
- **Range**: 21/100
- **Impact**: 30/100
- **Fire rate**: 65/100
- **Level**: 25
- **Added in**: Fresh Season 2023 ( 3.0.0 )
- **Other variant**: Clash Blaster

## Splatoon 2

- **Class**: Shooter
- **Sub**: Curling Bomb
- **Special**: Tenta Missiles
- **Special points**: 180p
- **Range**: 21/100
- **Impact**: 25/100
- **Fire rate**: 65/100
- **Level**: 30
- **Cost**: 20,500
- **Added in**: 7 April 2018
- **Base damage**: 15 (Splash) 30 (Blast) 60 (Direct hit)
- **Ink consumption**: 4%
- **Other variant**: Clash Blaster

## Appearance

The Clash Blaster Neo is nearly identical in appearance to the Clash Blaster .

In Splatoon 2 , the only difference between the two is that the Clash Blaster Neo has stickers bearing the Neo logo on the front and side.

In Splatoon 3 , the design remains the same, except that the Clash Blaster Neo now has a dark red body color compared to the original's light navy blue.

## Trivia

Before being officially announced, the Clash Blaster Neo appeared in the Splatoon 2 version 3.0 preview section of the Nintendo Direct on 8 March 2018 . It was shown to come with Burst Bombs and Tenta Missiles . However, later datamining and the final release have shown the weapon to come with Curling Bombs , in contradiction to the video.

In Splatoon 2 , the Clash Blaster Neo is one of the only three weapons to be unlocked simultaneously with its alternate kit, the Clash Blaster . The other pairs are the H-3 Nozzlenose D and Cherry H-3 Nozzlenose , and the E-liter 4K Scope and Custom E-liter 4K Scope . It however remains as the only completely unique weapon. This is due to the Clash Blaster being unlocked at Level 30, which is the highest level for unlocking weapons.

This is no longer true in Splatoon 3 , since the Clash Blaster Neo is unlocked three levels after the Clash Blaster.
