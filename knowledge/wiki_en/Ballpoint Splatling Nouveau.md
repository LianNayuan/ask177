# Ballpoint Splatling Nouveau

The Ballpoint Splatling Nouveau has a nearly consistent charge rate for both rings. It takes 0.83 seconds to reach the first level of charge, and 1.66 seconds in total to reach the second level of charge. Once the first level of charge is reached, it fires for approximately 1.66 seconds, and once the second level of charge is reached, it fires for approximately 3.33 seconds.

## Appearance

The Ballpoint Splatling Nouveau has the same appearance as the Ballpoint Splatling, but has a Nouveau sticker on the back end. In Splatoon 3 , its colors are also inverted, with the stock being white instead of black, and the grip being black instead of white.

## Trivia

The Ballpoint Splatling Nouveau has the longest name out of any weapon in Splatoon 2 , being 27 characters long. However, in Splatoon 3 the weapon with the longest name is the Heavy Edit Splatling Nouveau , being 28 characters long.

As of Drizzle Season 2023 , the Ballpoint Splatling Nouveau is the only weapon unlocked at the same level as its first kit, the Ballpoint Splatling , both being unlocked at level 30.
