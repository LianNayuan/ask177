## Appearance

The Octo Shot Replica bears resemblance to the original design of the Splattershot , albeit with the aesthetics of a firearm instead of a toy. The main parts of the gun are a black body with a pistol grip and trigger, a foregrip attached to the barrel, and an ink tank which changes color to match the user's ink. The ink tank is connected to the body via an elongated metal tube at the back (which sticks out much further than the Splattershot's rear tube) and a black strip wrapping around the top of the tank, attaching it to the body. On the end of the body is a gray metal nozzle.

## Trivia

The Octo Shot Replica is the only weapon given by Judd in Splatoon , as the rest of his rewards are gear .

In Splatoon 2 , the Octo Shot Replica is the only weapon besides the Splattershot Jr. that can be obtained by players at level 1. This also means the Octo Shot Replica can be obtained before the weapon it is an alternate skin of, the Tentatek Splattershot .
