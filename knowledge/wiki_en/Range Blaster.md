## Description

The Range Blaster, as its name implies, is a variation of a regular Blaster with shots that travel farther before exploding. As a tradeoff, this blaster has a lower fire rate and higher ink consumption.

## Appearance

In Splatoon and Splatoon 2 , the Range Blaster is similar in appearance to the Blaster . However, the front and back parts of the Range Blaster have been pushed further apart by some metal supports.

In Splatoon 3 , the frontal part is much larger, less cylinder-like, and partially covers the aforementioned metal supports. The red wires on the exposed part were removed, and four thick black tubes are instead connected from the ink tank to the main body. The tank on the back is also different and has a small red label on a yellow banner.

## Trivia

Despite being called the Range Blaster, the Rapid Blaster has more range.

The shell of the Range Blaster as it appears in Splatoon 3 may be closely inspired by the fuel tank of a Harley-Davidson motorcycle.
