# Splatana Stamper Nouveau

The Splatana Stamper Nouveau , formerly known as the Neo Splatana Stamper , is a main weapon in Splatoon 3 . It is the Nouveau -branded version of the Splatana Stamper , with identical stats.

## Splatoon 3

- **Class**: Splatana
- **Sub**: Toxic Mist
- **Special**: Crab Tank
- **Special points**: 210p
- **Range**: 75/100
- **Damage**: 43/100
- **Handling**: 60/100
- **Level**: 20
- **Added in**: Chill Season 2023 ( 6.0.0 )
- **Base damage**: 35 (Projectile-tap) 20 (Direct-tap) 70 (Projectile-charge) 140 (Direct-charge) 100 ( SRNW Projectile-tap) 45 ( SRNW Direct-tap) 180 ( SRNW Projectile-charge) 220 ( SRNW Direct-charge)
- **Ink consumption**: 5.2% (tap) 11.7% (hold)
- **Other variants**: Splatana Stamper Stickerz Splatana Stamper Order Splatana Replica

## Appearance

The Splatana Stamper Nouveau is similar to the appearance of the Splatana Stamper , except the base, which is white with a green strip along the handle. The date-wheel is also green, and the metal chassis has a sticker bearing the Nouveau logo.

## Trivia

The Splatana Stamper Nouveau is the first new weapon with Crab Tank to be added since the launch of Splatoon 3 .

The Splatana Stamper Nouveau is the only weapon to have its name changed significantly (unlike the Octo Shot which only had a space added to it), and the first weapon to have its name changed between versions of the same game (unlike the Octo Shot which was changed between Splatoon and Splatoon 2 ), due to the previous name erroneously referencing the wrong brand.
