# Order Shot Replica

The Order Shot Replica is a main weapon in Splatoon 3 . It is a reskin of the Splattershot .

## Splatoon 3

- **Class**: Shooter
- **Sub**: Suction Bomb
- **Special**: Trizooka
- **Special points**: 200p
- **Range**: 52/100
- **Damage**: 47/100
- **Fire rate**: 60/100
- **Level**: 0
- **Added in**: Side Order ( 7.0.0 )
- **Base damage**: 36
- **Other variants**: Splattershot Tentatek Splattershot Glamorz Splattershot Hero Shot Replica Octo Shot Replica

## Appearance

The Order Shot has a minimalist appearance, with a white molded piece for the barrel, ink tank, and handle of the weapon, as well as a metallic silver end cap and nozzle. Its appearance is similar to that of a Splattershot .
