# Custom Explosher

The Custom Explosher is a main weapon in Splatoon 2 and Splatoon 3 . It is a Custom -branded variant of the Explosher .

## Splatoon 3

- **Class**: Slosher
- **Sub**: Splash Wall
- **Special**: Triple Splashdown
- **Special points**: 190p
- **Range**: 77/100
- **Damage**: 65/100
- **Handling**: 20/100
- **Level**: 30
- **Added in**: Fresh Season 2024
- **Base damage**: 35 (Splash) 55 (Direct)
- **Other variant**: Explosher

## Splatoon 2

- **Class**: Slosher
- **Sub**: Point Sensor
- **Special**: Baller
- **Special points**: 220p
- **Range**: 77/100
- **Damage**: 65/100
- **Handling**: 20/100
- **Level**: 22
- **Cost**: 12,400
- **Added in**: 4.3.0
- **Base damage**: 35 (Splash) 55 (Direct hit)
- **Ink consumption**: 11.5%
- **Other variant**: Explosher

## Appearance

In Splatoon 2 , the Custom Explosher is nearly identical in appearance to the Explosher . The only difference between the two is that the Custom Explosher has a sticker bearing the Custom logo on its side.

In Splatoon 3 , the Custom sticker is still present, but the color of the weapon is beige instead of olive green.
