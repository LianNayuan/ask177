## Appearance

The Hero Shot Replica, like the Splattershot and Hero Shot , vastly differs in appearance between games.

In Splatoon , the Hero Shot Replica is identical to its game's Hero Shot with no upgrades, being mainly black and yellow with blue lights.

In Splatoon 2 , it is again identical to its relative game's Hero Shot, this time being mainly yellow with some black parts and being based on the Level 3 Hero Shot, which in turn resembles the real-life FN P90 .

In Splatoon 3 , it is also identical in appearance to the single-player's Hero Shot.
