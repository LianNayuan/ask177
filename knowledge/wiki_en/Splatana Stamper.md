# Splatana Stamper

The Splatana Stamper is a main weapon in Splatoon 3 , made by Nouveau .

- **Special points**: 210p

## Appearance

The Splatana Stamper resembles a chainsaw and elongated wheeled date stamp. Inside the metal chassis is a date wheel, attached by a tri-wing screw. The wheel rotates a line of square stamps to form a serrated blade. Each stamp has a square script character; these are written in alphabetical order. At the bottom of the chassis is an embossed square script label resembling a product serial, and attached is a holdable navy ink tank with the Nouveau logo printed on.

## Trivia

The Splatana Stamper's design was inspired by a date stamp provided by Nintendo to developers upon joining.

When attacking with the Splatana Stamper, the stamps on the wheel rotate. This is especially noticeable when charging it to perform a vertical slash.
