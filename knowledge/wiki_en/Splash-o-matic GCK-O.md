# Splash-o-matic GCK-O

The Splash-o-matic GCK-O is a main weapon in the Splatoon series . It is a Barazushi -branded variant of the Splash-o-matic .

## Splatoon 3

- **Class**: Shooter
- **Sub**: Toxic Mist
- **Special**: Ink Storm
- **Special points**: 180p
- **Range**: 43/100
- **Damage**: 22/100
- **Fire rate**: 75/100
- **Level**: 22
- **Cost**: 1
- **Added in**: Splatlands Collection ( 10.0.0 )
- **Other variants**: Splash-o-matic Neo Splash-o-matic

## Appearance

The Splash-o-matic GCK-O is nearly identical in appearance to the Splash-o-matic . However, parts of the weapon that are colored dull bronze are now dark brown, and it has a lamp attached on top. The Barazushi logo can be seen of the side of the weapon.

## Etymology

The suffix "GCK-O" is a stylized spelling of the word gecko .
