# Order Dualies

The Order Dualies are a pair of dualies in Splatoon 3 that are exclusively used in Side Order .

## Appearance

The Order Dualies are based on the Splat Dualies , but have a minimalist appearance, with a single white molded piece for the handle and body of the weapon, as well as a metallic silver ink tank and nozzle. When firing, ring-shaped holograms manifest around both Dualies.

## Side Order

The Order Dualies are the first available weapon in Side Order . Their base set consists of Curling Bombs and Reefslider . However, this can be changed in the Spire of Order by using a vending machine .

They have weaker stats in comparison to the Splat Dualies , with lower fire rate, damage, strafe speed, ink efficiency, range, and only one dodge roll. However, this can be upgraded through color chips and Marina's Hacks .

Pearl’s Palette is the first Palette in Side Order . It is available from the start and set to the Order Dualies. Additionally, it is the only available Palette during the initial climb of the Spire of Order . Defeating Overlorder with this Palette for the first time unlocks the Order Dualie Replicas for use in online battles.
