## Appearance

In Splatoon and Splatoon 2 , The Custom Jet Squelcher is nearly identical in appearance to the Jet Squelcher , with the only difference being that the Custom Jet Squelcher has a sticker on the bottom part bearing the Custom logo.

In Splatoon 3 , the color of the Custom Jet Squelcher's nozzle has been changed from dull white to yellow.
