# E-liter 4K Scope

The E-liter 4K Scope is an E-liter 4K with a scope attached. Compared to the scopeless version it has enhanced range and a zoomed view for greater aiming precision, but players have their field of vision limited by the scope, and it can not store its charge in swim form . Besides this, all other stats are the same as the E-liter 4K , and it shares its sub and special weapons.

## Appearance

The E-liter 4K Scope is nearly identical in appearance to the E-liter 4K . The only difference between the two is that the E-liter 4K Scope has a scope attached to the gas pump handle and the back end of the barrel.
