# Rapid Blaster Pro WNT-R

The Rapid Blaster Pro WNT-R is a main weapon in the Splatoon series . It is a Barazushi -branded variant of the the Rapid Blaster Pro .

## Splatoon 3

- **Class**: Blaster
- **Sub**: Suction Bomb
- **Special**: Tacticooler
- **Special points**: 200p
- **Range**: 72/100
- **Impact**: 35/100
- **Fire rate**: 30/100
- **Level**: 30
- **Added in**: Splatlands Collection ( 10.0.0 )
- **Other variants**: Rapid Blaster Pro Rapid Blaster Pro Deco

## Appearance

Compared to the Rapid Blaster Pro , the Rapid Blaster Pro WNT-R features new colors: the barrel and hand guard, usually red, are now light brown, while the other parts, usually red, are now silver, as is the barrel tip. The illuminated parts of the weapon do not change color to match the user's ink, remaining orange. The white parts of the weapon are now gray, with a camouflage texture and the addition of the Barazushi logo. Finally, a kind of red seaweed is attached to the side of the weapon.

## Trivia

This weapon shares a "winter"-theme appearance with three other Splatlands Collection weapons from Barazushi : the Splattershot Pro FRZ-N , Splat Charger CAM-O , and Splatterscope CAM-O . In the original Japanese version of the game, the Splat Charger CAM-O and its scoped variant have the suffix "FRS-T", which most likely refers to the English word "Frost". They also share a similar color scheme and also have a single piece of red kelp attached to them.

## Etymology

The suffix "WNTR" is a stylized spelling of the word winter .
