# Order Charger Replica

The Order Charger Replica is a main weapon in Splatoon 3 . It is a reskin of the Splat Charger , and a direct successor to the Splatoon 2 Hero Charger Replica .

## Splatoon 3

- **Class**: Charger
- **Sub**: Splat Bomb
- **Special**: Ink Vac
- **Special points**: 190p
- **Range**: 88/100
- **Charge speed**: 50/100
- **Mobility**: 40/100
- **Level**: 0
- **Added in**: Side Order ( 7.0.0 )
- **Base damage**: 40–80 (Partial charge) 160 (Full charge)
- **Other variants**: Splat Charger Z+F Splat Charger Splat Charger CAM-O

## Appearance

The Order Charger Replica is designed to look like a simplified version of the Splat Charger , containing only white and gray colors.

## Trivia

Along with the Hero Charger Replica , the Order Charger Replica is the only variant of the Splat Charger to not have a scoped version .
