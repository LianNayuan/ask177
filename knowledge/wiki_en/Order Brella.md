# Order Brella

The Order Brella is a brella in Splatoon 3 that is exclusively used in Side Order .

## Appearance

The Order Brella is based on the Splat Brella , but has a minimalist appearance, with a single white molded piece for the handle of the weapon, as well as a metallic silver canopy. When firing, ring-shaped holograms manifest around the base of the Brella.

## Side Order

The Order Brella is a weapon unlocked in Side Order . Its base set consists of Sprinklers and Ink Storm , notably identical to the Splat Brella 's set in Splatoon 2 . However, this can be changed in the Spire of Order by using a vending machine .

It has weaker stats in comparison to the Splat Brella , with a slower fire rate, strafe speed, ink efficiency, and range, but has higher contact damage and faster launch and cooldown time. However, this can be upgraded through color chips and Marina's Hacks .

Marina's Palette is the second Palette in Side Order . It is available after completing the first climb of the Spire of Order and is set to the Order Brella. After the first climb , Marina gives it to the player upon their approach to the spire. Defeating Overlorder with this Palette for the first time unlocks the Order Brella Replica for use in online battles.
