# Order Blaster

The Order Blaster is a blaster in Splatoon 3 that is exclusively used in Side Order .

## Appearance

The Order Blaster is based on the Luna Blaster , but has a minimalist appearance, with a single white molded piece for the barrel and handle of the weapon, as well as metallic silver front and back balls. When firing, ring-shaped holograms manifest around the nozzle and base of the blaster.

## Side Order

The Order Blaster is a weapon unlocked in Side Order . Its base set consists of Curling Bombs and Wave Breaker . However, this can be changed in the Spire of Order by using a vending machine .

It has lower stats in comparison to the Luna Blaster, with a slower fire rate, explosion damage, strafe speed, ink efficiency, range, and blast radius. However, this can be upgraded through color chips and Marina's Hacks .

## Murch's Palette

Murch's Palette is one of the twelve Palettes in Side Order . It can be found in one of the lockers in front of the Spire of Order and is set to the Order Blaster. Defeating Overlorder with this Palette for the first time unlocks the Order Blaster Replica for use in online battles.
