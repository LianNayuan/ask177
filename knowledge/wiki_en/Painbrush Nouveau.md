# Painbrush Nouveau

The Painbrush Nouveau is a main weapon in Splatoon 3 . It is the Nouveau -branded version of the Painbrush .

## Splatoon 3

- **Class**: Brush
- **Sub**: Point Sensor
- **Special**: Tenta Missiles
- **Special points**: 190p
- **Range**: 33/100
- **Ink speed**: 85/100
- **Handling**: 60/100
- **Level**: 16
- **Added in**: Chill Season 2023 ( 6.0.0 )
- **Base damage**: 31-60 (Splash) 30 (Roll)
- **Ink consumption**: 4.8% (Flinging ink) 10.8% per second (Sliding)
- **Other variants**: Painbrush Painbrush BRN-Z

## Appearance

The Painbrush Nouveau looks similar to the Painbrush, however it has brown bristles and a gold tint similar to that of the Gold Dynamo Roller . It has a Nouveau sticker wrapped around the handle just below the brush head. The ring separating the handle from the rest of the weapon never changes color, unlike the original version. The handle itself is a much darker wood than the original Painbrush.

## Etymology

"Nouveau" is the brand which created the original Painbrush.
