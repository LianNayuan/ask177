## Appearance

The Sloshing Machine Neo is nearly identical in appearance to the Sloshing Machine . The only difference between the two is that the Sloshing Machine Neo has a sticker bearing the Neo logo on its side. In Splatoon 3 the place of the logo is changed to be on top of the weapon, and the bottom part of it is now black.

## Trivia

In Splatoon , at 28,500 cash , the Sloshing Machine Neo is the most expensive slosher and the third most expensive weapon .

The Sloshing Machine Neo has the Point Sensor as its sub weapon in all three Splatoon games.

## Etymology

Sloshing Machine Neo is a pun derived from the verb to slosh and Washing Machine , plus the suffix Neo , Ancient Greek for new . The suffix Neo is also used to differentiate it from the Sloshing Machine . This trait is shared among the other alternate sets of the Neo brand.

スクリュースロッシャーネオ Sukuryū Surosshā Neo is derived from Screw and the verb to slosh , plus the suffix Neo . Screw likely refers to spinning motion of this weapon's projectile. Like the English name, Neo is also used to differentiate it from Screw Slosher.
