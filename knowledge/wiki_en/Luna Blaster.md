# Luna Blaster

The Luna Blaster is a main weapon in the Splatoon series , created by Neo . It is a blaster with short range but above average fire rate, blast radius, and ink efficiency.

## Description

Like other blasters , the Luna Blaster fires an explosive round that lets it deal damage to opponents hiding behind obstacles or around corners. It takes one direct hit or two indirect hits to splat an opponent, but shooting the opponent directly rather than relying on the blast takes careful and precise aim due to the weapon's low firing rate. However, out of all blasters that can splat opponents in one direct hit, the Luna Blaster actually has the largest blast size and fastest fire rate, at the cost of having the shortest range.

## Appearance

The Luna Blaster's main body is round and smooth with a hole at the end to let out blaster shots. The back half is white, while the front half changes color to match the user's ink in all games. The back half houses a handhold with the trigger on it; the trigger changes to match the user's ink color. Sticking out of the back of the Luna Blaster is a ball on the end of a thin stick (white in Splatoon , matches the user's ink color in Splatoon 2 and Splatoon 3 ), and an attachment that is similar but bigger (matches the user's ink color in Splatoon , white in Splatoon 2 and Splatoon 3 ) is placed above the hole in the front. In Splatoon , there is a Neo logo written in Inkling on the back half. In Splatoon 2 , the text is replaced by a faint Neo logo icon. In Splatoon 3 , the parts of its casing that match the user's ink color are now semi transparent, revealing its internal components. The Neo logo is replaced by small text in gray, and there is a small seam dividing the white part of the casing into two pieces.

Additionally, in Splatoon 3 , the Luna Blaster features considerably more detailed effects when firing: the colored parts of the blaster will illuminate, and the casing will split into three parts (the fore colored part, and two white parts divided along the aforementioned seam).

## Trivia

In Splatoon , the Luna Blaster and its variant, as well as the Inkbrush and its variants have the shortest range stat in the game. This continues in Splatoon 2 , except the Inkbrush has slightly less range.

The Luna Blaster appears to be a jet turbine; with the protruding parts representing the oxygen intake.

The Luna Blaster was one of the weapons that were available to use in the Splatoon 3 Splatfest World Premiere .

In Splatoon 3 , the Luna Blaster was the first blaster to have its second variant debuted.
