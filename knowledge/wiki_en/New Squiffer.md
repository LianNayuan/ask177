## Appearance

In Splatoon and Splatoon 2 , the New Squiffer is nearly identical in appearance to the Classic Squiffer . The only difference between the two is that the New Squiffer has a red and yellow starburst sticker with some white Inkling text stuck on the bottle.

In Splatoon 3 , the New Squiffer features a new thinner, rectangular bottle design. As opposed to the usual blue of the Classic Squiffer , the New Squiffer features a more green color. The red and yellow sticker is still present, however.

## Trivia

Like the Classic Squiffer , the New Squiffer appears to be designed after a spray bottle. The "New" sticker seems to be designed after similar to packaging callouts used in marketing of new or improved formulas for cleaning products and food items.

The New Squiffer, along with the Classic Squiffer , are the only chargers in the game with the ability to mid air charge without a penalty as of Splatoon 3 version 7.0.0 . With this unique trait it allows for the New Squiffer to attack enemies mid air with its Zipcaster .
