# Bamboozler 14 Mk II

The Bamboozler 14 Mk II is a main weapon in the Splatoon series . It is a Bamboozler 14 Mk I with the Cuttlegear logo plastered onto it and a different loadout.

## Appearance

The Bamboozler 14 Mk II is nearly identical to the Bamboozler 14 Mk I . The only difference between the two is that the Bamboozler 14 Mk II has a sticker bearing the Cuttlegear logo on the back of the barrel.

In Splatoon 3 , the Bamboozler 14 Mk II features a cooler green for the color of the bamboo.

## Trivia

With a full set of Damage Up , this weapon can reach 99.99 damage with less than a full charge. Since the range is the same no matter the charge, this can prove useful.
