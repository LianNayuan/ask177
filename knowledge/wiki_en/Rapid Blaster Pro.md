# Rapid Blaster Pro

The Rapid Blaster Pro is a Rapid Blaster with an elongated handle and barrel, and increased range to match. Its range is the greatest of all blasters. As a trade-off, it has a slightly slower fire rate and higher ink consumption compared to the Rapid Blaster. Damage output and blast size are identical for both weapons.

## Appearance

The Rapid Blaster Pro is similar in appearance to the Rapid Blaster . However, the nozzle is longer, and there is a gray bar connecting the red panels at the bottom to the handle.

## Trivia

This weapon resembles a toy dart blaster.

Prior to version 3.0.0 of Splatoon 3 , the colored parts on the 2D icons for the Rapid Blaster Pro and Rapid Blaster were colored yellow. This was corrected to the white color upon release of the update.
