# Jet Squelcher COB-R

The Jet Squelcher COB-R is a main weapon in the Splatoon series . It is a Barazushi -branded variant of the the Jet Squelcher from the Splatlands Collection and features a different sub and special weapon .

## Splatoon 3

- **Class**: Shooter
- **Sub**: Burst Bomb
- **Special**: Triple Splashdown
- **Special points**: 190p
- **Range**: 82/100
- **Damage**: 35/100
- **Fire rate**: 30/100
- **Level**: 21
- **Added in**: Splatlands Collection ( 10.0.0 )
- **Other variants**: Jet Squelcher Custom Jet Squelcher

## Appearance

The Jet Squelcher COB-R is nearly identical in appearance to the Jet Squelcher . However, parts of the weapon that are colored dark blue are now sporting a light brown scale pattern, referencing a cobra which the weapon is named after.

## Etymology

The suffix "COB-R" is a stylized spelling of the word cobra , regarding the weapon's snake skin-like texture.
