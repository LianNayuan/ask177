# Order Roller

The Order Roller is a roller in Splatoon 3 that is exclusively used in Side Order .

## Appearance

The Order Roller is based on the Splat Roller , but has a minimalist appearance, with a white molded support and ink tank for the body of the weapon, as well as a metallic silver roller and handle. When used, ring-shaped holograms manifest around the ends of the rolling part of the roller.

## Side Order

The Order Roller is a weapon unlocked in Side Order . Its base set consists of Burst Bombs and Kraken Royale . However, this can be changed in the Spire of Order by using a vending machine .

It has lower stats in comparison to the Splat Roller , with a slower swing time, damage, strafe speed, rolling speed, ink efficiency, ink coverage, and range. However, this can be upgraded through color chips and Marina's Hacks .

Callie's Palette is one of the twelve Palettes in Side Order . It can be found in one of the lockers in front of the Spire of Order and is set to the Order Roller. Defeating Overlorder with this Palette for the first time unlocks the Order Roller Replica for use in online battles.
