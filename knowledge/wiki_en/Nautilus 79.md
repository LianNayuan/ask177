# Nautilus 79

The Nautilus 79 is a main weapon in Splatoon 2 and Splatoon 3 . It is a variant of the Nautilus 47 with a different look and loadout.

## Splatoon 3

- **Class**: Splatling
- **Sub**: Suction Bomb
- **Special**: Triple Splashdown
- **Special points**: 200p
- **Range**: 74/100
- **Charge speed**: 37/100
- **Mobility**: 70/100
- **Level**: 29
- **Added in**: Fresh Season 2024 ( 7.0.0 )
- **Base damage**: 32
- **Other variant**: Nautilus 47

## Splatoon 2

- **Other variant**: Nautilus 47

## Appearance

The Nautilus 79 is similar in appearance to the Nautilus 47 . However, all the metal parts are slightly darker, and the rim of the shell and the handhold are outlined in gold.

## Trivia

According to dataminers, in Splatoon 2 , the Nautilus 79's kit consisted of Splat Bomb and Ink Armor prior to its release, but this was changed upon its release in version 4.3.0 .

## Etymology

Nautilus refers to the weapon's resemblance to the shell of a mollusc of the same name and 79 is the atomic number of gold, referring the weapon's gold coloration.
