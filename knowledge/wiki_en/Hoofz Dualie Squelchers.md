# Hoofz Dualie Squelchers

The Hoofz Dualie Squelchers is a main weapon in Splatoon 3 , and is the Emberz -branded version of the Dualie Squelchers . It was added as part of the Splatlands Collection on 12 June 2025.

## Splatoon 3

- **Class**: Dualie
- **Sub**: Point Sensor
- **Special**: Splattercolor Screen
- **Special points**: 190p
- **Range**: 70/100
- **Damage**: 22/100
- **Mobility**: 70/100
- **Level**: 17
- **Added in**: Splatlands Collection ( 10.0.0 )
- **Other variants**: Dualie Squelchers Custom Dualie Squelchers

## Appearance

The Hoofz Dualie Squelchers resemble the Dualie Squelchers but with a new color scheme. The insides of the Dualie Squelchers are now a light blue denim pattern while the outsides have a black and white color pattern similar to a zebra. The right Dualie Squelcher has a ring with six spikes added on the nozzle.

## Etymology

The word hoofz is a stylized spelling of the word hooves , which matches the zebra-print pattern on the weapon bodies thematically.
