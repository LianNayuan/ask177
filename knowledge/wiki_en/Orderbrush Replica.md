# Orderbrush Replica

The Orderbrush Replica is a main weapon in Splatoon 3 .

## Splatoon 3

- **Class**: Brush
- **Sub**: Suction Bomb
- **Special**: Zipcaster
- **Special points**: 190p
- **Range**: 23/100
- **Ink speed**: 80/100
- **Handling**: 85/100
- **Level**: 0
- **Added in**: Side Order ( 7.0.0 )
- **Base damage**: 20-40 (Splash) 25 (Roll)
- **Other variants**: Octobrush Octobrush Nouveau Cometz Octobrush

## Appearance

The Orderbrush Replica is designed to look like a simplified version of the Octobrush , containing only white and gray colors.
