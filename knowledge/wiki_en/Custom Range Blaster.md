The Custom Range Blaster is a main weapon in the Splatoon series . It is a Custom -branded version of the Range Blaster .

## Appearance

The Custom Range Blaster is nearly identical in appearance to the Range Blaster . The only difference between the two is that the Custom Range Blaster has a sticker bearing the Custom logo on the front part.

## Trivia

In Splatoon 3 , the Custom Range Blaster is the only weapon variation produced by Custom to not have any recolored parts that would distinguish it as a variation of the Range Blaster .
