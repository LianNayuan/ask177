## Appearance

The H-3 Nozzlenose D in Splatoon and Splatoon 2 is nearly identical in appearance to the H-3 Nozzlenose, with the only difference being the presence of an added decal of the dolphin logo associated with its brand on the hose.

In Splatoon 3 , the H-3 Nozzlenose D has undergone a noticeable visual change, setting it further apart from its standard counterpart: its build is now significantly lighter in color, with most of the frame becoming white and all of the darker blue elements shifting to a significantly lighter shade. It retains the dolphin decal.

## Etymology

The appended "D" in this weapon's name likely stands for "Dolphin", matching both the logo of its manufacturer and the dolphin theme of the base weapon's name in some languages.
