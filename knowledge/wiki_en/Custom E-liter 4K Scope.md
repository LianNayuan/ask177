# Custom E-liter 4K Scope

The Custom E-liter 4K Scope is a main weapon in Splatoon 2 and Splatoon 3 . It is the scoped version of the Custom E-liter 4K .

## Splatoon 3

- **Class**: Charger
- **Sub**: Squid Beakon
- **Special**: Kraken Royale
- **Special points**: 210p
- **Range**: 100/100
- **Charge speed**: 20/100
- **Mobility**: 5/100
- **Level**: 28
- **Added in**: Fresh Season 2024
- **Base damage**: 40–80 (Partial charge) 180 (Full charge)
- **Other variant**: E-liter 4K Scope

## Splatoon 2

- **Class**: Charger
- **Sub**: Squid Beakon
- **Special**: Bubble Blower
- **Special points**: 170p
- **Range**: 100/100
- **Charge speed**: 20/100
- **Mobility**: 5/100
- **Level**: 30
- **Cost**: 29,900
- **Added in**: 14 October 2017
- **Base damage**: 40–80 (Partial charge) 180 (Full charge)
- **Ink consumption**: 2.25% (No charge) 25% (Full charge)
- **Other variant**: E-liter 4K Scope

## Appearance

The Custom E-liter 4K Scope is nearly identical in appearance to the Custom E-liter 4K . The only difference between the two is that the Custom E-liter 4K Scope has a scope attached to the back end of the barrel.

## Trivia

At 29,900 , the Custom E-Liter 4K Scope is the most expensive charger and the third most expensive weapon in Splatoon 2 .
