# Custom Douser Dualies FF

The Custom Douser Dualies FF are main weapons in Splatoon 3 . It is a Custom branded variant of the Douser Dualies FF with a different kit and design.

## Splatoon 3

- **Class**: Dualie
- **Sub**: Burst Bomb
- **Special**: Triple Inkstrike
- **Special points**: 200p
- **Level**: 27
- **Added in**: Sizzle Season 2024 ( 8.0.0 )
- **Other variant**: Douser Dualies FF

## Appearance

The weapon is a redesigned Douser Dualies FF , featuring yellow and blue colors instead of the traditional red and green. Custom logo stickers have been placed on both barrels, however the one on the left-hand dualie is mirrored.
