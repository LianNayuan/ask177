# Clawz .96 Gal

The Clawz .96 Gal was revealed on June 10th, 2025, in the "Splatoon Raiders – Announcement Trailer & More" video on the Nintendo of America YouTube channel. The weapon was first made available in Splatoon 3' s 10.0.0 update on June 12th, 2025.

## Appearance

The Clawz .96 Gal's design features prominent leopard-spot patterns on the outer sections of the main reservoir, which lend the weapon its name. The grip and middle section of the main reservoir are colored periwinkle, with the middle section of the base bearing the Emberz logo. The barrel of the weapon features the same glass design from the game's original .96 Gal, with the addition of six spikes protruding from the rim at the end of the barrel.

## Etymology

The word clawz is a stylized spelling of the word claws , which matches the leopard-spot pattern on the main reservoir thematically.
