## Appearance

The main part of the Splattershot Pro seems to be a large, crayon-shaped canister that changes color to match the user's ink . Over the top of this is a gray and blue mechanism that keeps the canister inserted, and around the canister are two gray straps that connect it to the weapon's frame. The back of the canister has a blue cap with a red tube. Attached to the trigger piece is a dark blue handhold that connects to the identically colored nozzle. The tip of the nozzle and trigger are red.

## Trivia

The Splattershot Pro was one of the weapons that was available to use in the Splatoon 3 Splatfest World Premiere .

Its design seems to be inspired by older Super Soaker Models with the 1998 CPS 1000 and the 1996 CPS 2000 sharing several similarities.

When Angle Shooter and Crab Tank come up on Judd's trials , the player is equipped with the Splattershot Pro on both occasions.

## Etymology

Splattershot Pro is a combination of splatter (as well as splat ) and shot . It also has the shortened form of professional at the end to denote it is a weapon for advanced players.

Its Japanese name, プライムシューター puraimu shūtā , means "Prime Shooter".
