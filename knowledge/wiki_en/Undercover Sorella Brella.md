# Undercover Sorella Brella

The Undercover Sorella Brella is a main weapon in Splatoon 2 & Splatoon 3 . It is a Sorella -branded version of the Undercover Brella and comes with a different sub weapon and special weapon .

## Splatoon 3

- **Class**: Brella
- **Sub**: Torpedo
- **Special**: Splattercolor Screen
- **Special points**: 180p
- **Range**: 50/100
- **Damage**: 30/100
- **Durability**: 25/100
- **Level**: 23
- **Added in**: Chill Season 2023 ( 6.0.0 )
- **Other variants**: Undercover Brella Patternz Undercover Brella

## Splatoon 2

- **Class**: Brella
- **Sub**: Splat Bomb
- **Special**: Baller
- **Special points**: 180p
- **Range**: 50/100
- **Damage**: 30/100
- **Durability**: 25/100
- **Level**: 19
- **Cost**: 11,900
- **Added in**: 1 June 2018
- **Base damage**: 12 per pellet (Cap: 40) 15 (Contact)
- **Ink consumption**: 4%
- **Other variants**: Undercover Brella Kensa Undercover Brella

## Appearance

The Undercover Sorella Brella is similar in appearance to the Undercover Brella . The handle is lighter in color and the shield has a black-and-gray checkered pattern instead of being pure black. In Splatoon 3 , certain lines in the checkered pattern as well as the shaft between the handle and endpoint of the umbrella changes based on the user's ink color.
