# Stickerz Splatana Stamper

The Stickerz Splatana Stamper is a main weapon in Splatoon 3 . It is an Emberz -branded version of the Splatana Stamper .

## Splatoon 3

- **Class**: Splatana
- **Sub**: Autobomb
- **Special**: Booyah Bomb
- **Special points**: 190p
- **Range**: 75/100
- **Damage**: 43/100
- **Handling**: 60/100
- **Level**: 22
- **Added in**: Splatlands Collection ( 10.0.0 )
- **Other variants**: Splatana Stamper Splatana Stamper Nouveau Order Splatana Replica

## Appearance

The Stickerz Splatana Stamper looks like the Splatana Stamper, but the handle is bright yellow and the blade is darker with stickers on it, including the Emberz logo.

## Etymology

The word stickerz is a stylized spelling of the word stickers .
