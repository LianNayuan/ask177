# Big Swig Roller Express

The Big Swig Roller Express is a main weapon in Splatoon 3 . It is an Express -branded variant of the Big Swig Roller .

## Splatoon 3

- **Class**: Roller
- **Sub**: Angle Shooter
- **Special**: Ink Storm
- **Special points**: 180p
- **Range**: 56/100
- **Ink speed**: 54/100
- **Handling**: 60/100
- **Level**: 18
- **Added in**: Sizzle Season 2023 ( 4.0.0 )
- **Other variants**: Big Swig Roller Planetz Big Swig Roller

## Appearance

The Big Swig Roller Express shares its overall design with its basic counterpart. However, the lavender parts of the frame are changed to yellow on the Big Swig Roller Express, and the Express logo is visible both above the joint piece as a sticker, and on the paper cup used as ink tank.

## Trivia

Along with the Annaki Splattershot Nova , the Big Swig Roller Express is the only weapon made by the Splat brand that has the actual name of the brand it was created with in almost every translation of the game instead of suffixes meaning "logo" or "branded" like the other Splat weapons. Coincidentally, the base forms of both of these weapons both come from Chill Season 2022 .

The Big Swig Roller Express is the first and only weapon to be a variant in collaboration with a brand that is not a gear brand , as is the case with other branded variants in the series.
