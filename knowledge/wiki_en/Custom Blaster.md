## Appearance

The Custom Blaster is nearly identical in appearance to the Blaster . The only difference between the two is that the Custom Blaster has a sticker bearing the Custom logo on the front part. In Splatoon 3 the Custom Blaster has two stickers, one on the top and the other on the bottom. The wires have also been changed from red to white.

## Trivia

In all three games, the Custom Blaster has a white Custom sticker on the barrel. However, in the first two, the weapon's icon has a matching white Custom logo, while in Splatoon 3 the logo is yellow.
