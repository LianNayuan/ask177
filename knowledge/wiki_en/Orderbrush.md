# Orderbrush

The Orderbrush is a brush in Splatoon 3 that is exclusively used in Side Order .

## Appearance

The Orderbrush is based on the Octobrush , but has a minimalist appearance, with a white molded support and bristles for the body of the weapon, as well as a metallic silver handle. When used, ring-shaped holograms manifest around the handle of the brush.

## Side Order

The Orderbrush is a weapon unlocked in Side Order . Its base set consists of Ink Mines and Ultra Stamp , notably identical to the Inkbrush Nouveau 's set in Splatoon 3 . However, this can be changed in the Spire of Order by using a vending machine .

It has lower stats in comparison to the Octobrush, with a slower ink flick, strafe speed, brush speed, ink efficiency, ink coverage, and range, but has higher damage. However, this can be upgraded through color chips and Marina's Hacks .

Sheldon's Palette is one of the twelve Palettes in Side Order . It can be found in one of the lockers in front of the Spire of Order and is set to the Orderbrush. Defeating Overlorder with this Palette for the first time unlocks the Orderbrush Replica for use in online battles.
