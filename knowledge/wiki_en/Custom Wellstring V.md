# Custom Wellstring V

The Custom Wellstring V is a main weapon in Splatoon 3 , made by Custom . It is the Custom branded version of the Wellstring V .

## Splatoon 3

- **Class**: Stringer
- **Sub**: Point Sensor
- **Special**: Wave Breaker
- **Special points**: 200p
- **Range**: 83/100
- **Charge speed**: 33/100
- **Mobility**: 30/100
- **Level**: 24
- **Added in**: Sizzle Season 2024 ( 8.0.0 )
- **Base damage**: 24-35(x5) (Direct) 30(x5) (Projectile explosion)
- **Other variant**: Wellstring V

## Appearance

The only distinctions from the Wellstring V are a full-colour Custom logo on the underside of the weapon, overlaid on top of the usual engraved logo, and its grip is black instead of light blue.

## Trivia

The Custom Wellstring V and the Wellstring V are the only stringers capable of splatting an opponent with one shot at no charge.
