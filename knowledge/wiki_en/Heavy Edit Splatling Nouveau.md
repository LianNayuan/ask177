# Heavy Edit Splatling Nouveau

The Heavy Edit Splatling Nouveau is a main weapon in Splatoon 3 . It is the Nouveau -branded version of the Heavy Edit Splatling .

## Splatoon 3

- **Class**: Splatling
- **Sub**: Splat Bomb
- **Special**: Crab Tank
- **Special points**: 210p
- **Range**: 70/100
- **Charge speed**: 14/100
- **Mobility**: 90/100
- **Level**: 17
- **Added in**: Sizzle Season 2024 ( 8.0.0 )
- **Base damage**: 26
- **Other variant**: Heavy Edit Splatling

## Appearance

The Heavy Edit Splatling Nouveau is broadly similar to its base weapon in appearance. The principal differences relate to its ammo box; it is in an opaque, metallic version of the wielder's ink color instead of semi-translucent white and features a Nouveau sticker in place of the previous embossed wordmark.

## Trivia

The Heavy Edit Splatling Nouveau has the longest name out of any weapon in Splatoon 3 , being 28 characters long.
