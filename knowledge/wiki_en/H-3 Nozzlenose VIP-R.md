# H-3 Nozzlenose VIP-R

The H-3 Nozzlenose VIP-R is a main weapon in the Splatoon series . It is a Barazushi -branded variant of the H-3 Nozzlenose .

## Splatoon 3

- **Class**: Shooter
- **Sub**: Suction Bomb
- **Special**: Triple Inkstrike
- **Special points**: 200p
- **Range**: 70/100
- **Damage**: 64/100
- **Fire rate**: 30/100
- **Level**: 29
- **Added in**: Splatlands Collection ( 10.0.0 )
- **Base damage**: 45
- **Other variants**: H-3 Nozzlenose H-3 Nozzlenose D

## Appearance

The H-3 Nozzlenose VIP-R is broadly similar in appearance to the H-3 Nozzlenose, but takes on various green colorations instead of the usual blue. The grips of both the weapon and its valve lever as well as part of the nozzle head all have a finish of green snake scales, and the reel and hose are a flat green and green-tinted, respectively. An orange-brown rubber strap fastens a jagged metal piece to the underside of the nozzle head, while a Barazushi brand decal is visible attached to the side of the reel, similar to the decals found on other variants such as the H-3 Nozzlenose D .

## Trivia

The line "Make your opponents dance" in the announcement Tweet for the weapon is a nod to the practice of snake charming .

The H-3 Nozzlenose VIP-R shares a snake-like design theme with the Jet Squelcher COB-R and Mini Splatling RTL-R . All three have a scaly texture and webbing with a metal piece at the end of the barrel.

## Etymology

The suffix "VIP-R" is a stylized spelling of the word viper .
