# Inkline Tri-Stringer

The Inkline Tri-Stringer is a main weapon in Splatoon 3 . It is an Inkline -branded version of the Tri-Stringer .

## Splatoon 3

- **Class**: Stringer
- **Sub**: Sprinkler
- **Special**: Super Chump
- **Special points**: 190p
- **Range**: 88/100
- **Charge speed**: 40/100
- **Mobility**: 40/100
- **Level**: 11
- **Added in**: Drizzle Season 2023 ( 5.0.0 )
- **Other variants**: Tri-Stringer Bulbz Tri-Stringer Order Stringer Replica

## Appearance

The Inkline Tri-Stringer mostly shares its appearance with the Tri-Stringer , except that the fishing rod is made out of a different material and has the Inkline logo emblazoned on one side. The nozzles and the center tube's cap are also colored olive green instead of black.

## Trivia

The Inkline Tri-Stringer is the second weapon with the Inkline branding, after the Tenta Sorella Brella .

Contrary to its final name, the Inkline Tri-Stringer was initially labeled as the 'Tri-Stringer Collab' on its Tableturf Battle card in the Drizzle Season 2023 trailer (a naming convention sometimes used generically for collaborative weapon variants in other languages). Whether this naming was in error or simply a placeholder is unknown.

If weapons from Splatoon 2 ' s Kensa Collection are excluded, the Inkline Tri-Stringer is the only weapon with gear branding to not be made by the Splat brand.
