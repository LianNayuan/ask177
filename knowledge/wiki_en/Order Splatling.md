# Order Splatling

The Order Splatling is a splatling in Splatoon 3 that is exclusively used in Side Order .

## Appearance

The Order Splatling is based on the Heavy Splatling , but has a minimalist appearance, with a white molded piece for the barrel and handle of the weapon, as well as a metallic silver ink tank and nozzle. When firing, ring-shaped holograms manifest around the nozzles of the splatling.

## Side Order

The Order Splatling is a weapon unlocked in Side Order . Its base set consists of Splash Walls and Booyah Bomb . However, this can be changed in the Spire of Order by using a vending machine .

It has lower stats in comparison to the Heavy Splatling, with lower damage, strafe speed, ink efficiency, range, and charge time. However, this can be upgraded through color chips and Marina's Hacks .

DJ Octavio's Palette is one of the twelve Palettes in Side Order . It can be found in one of the lockers in front of the Spire of Order and is set to the Order Splatling. Defeating Overlorder with this Palette for the first time unlocks the Order Splatling Replica for use in online battles.
