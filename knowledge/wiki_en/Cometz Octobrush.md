# Cometz Octobrush

The Cometz Octobrush is a main weapon in Splatoon 3 , and is the Emberz -branded version of the Octobrush . It was added as part of the Splatlands Collection on 12 June 2025.

## Splatoon 3

- **Class**: Brush
- **Sub**: Autobomb
- **Special**: Kraken Royale
- **Special points**: 190p
- **Range**: 23/100
- **Ink speed**: 80/100
- **Handling**: 85/100
- **Level**: 11
- **Added in**: Splatlands Collection ( 10.0.0 )
- **Other variants**: Octobrush Octobrush Nouveau Orderbrush Replica

## Appearance

The Cometz Octobrush shares its overall design with the Octobrush . However, the ink reservoir, the tip of the handle, and the upper part of the bristles holder are a tempered steel with a strong blue/purple tint, the anchor wires are instead the Emberz logo colors: neon red, neon green, and neon blue, the text version of the Emberz logo is visible at the user-side of the anchor wires on the upper part, there are 3 bulbs attached to the right side of the ink reservoir in the Emberz logo colors, the rest of the frame is marble and, except for the ink color, the bristles are a black color.
