# Annaki Splattershot Nova

The Annaki Splattershot Nova is a main weapon in Splatoon 3 , and is the Annaki -branded version of the Splattershot Nova .

## Splatoon 3

- **Class**: Shooter
- **Sub**: Ink Mine
- **Special**: Inkjet
- **Special points**: 210p
- **Range**: 68/100
- **Damage**: 10/100
- **Fire rate**: 60/100
- **Level**: 12
- **Added in**: Sizzle Season 2023 ( 4.0.0 )
- **Other variant**: Splattershot Nova

## Appearance

The Annaki Splattershot Nova features the same design as the regular Splattershot Nova , but comes with raspberry-colored accents instead of the original weapon's lemon-yellow. It also has an Annaki logo on both sides of the front half that change to match the user's ink color.

## Trivia

Along with the Big Swig Roller Express , the Annaki Splattershot Nova is the only weapon made by the Splat brand that has the actual name of the brand it was created with in almost every translation of the game instead of suffixes meaning "logo" or "branded" like the other Splat weapons. Coincidentally, the base forms of both of these weapons both come from the Chill Season 2022 .
