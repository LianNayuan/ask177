# Painbrush BRN-Z

The Painbrush BRN-Z is a main weapon in Splatoon 3 . It is a Barazushi -branded variant of the Painbrush .

## Splatoon 3

- **Class**: Brush
- **Sub**: Splash Wall
- **Special**: Trizooka
- **Special points**: 190p
- **Range**: 33/100
- **Ink speed**: 85/100
- **Handling**: 60/100
- **Level**: 18
- **Added in**: Splatlands Collection ( 10.0.0 )
- **Base damage**: 31-60 (Splash) 30 (Roll)
- **Ink consumption**: 4.8% (Flinging ink) 10.8% per second (Sliding)
- **Other variants**: Painbrush Painbrush Nouveau

## Appearance

The Painbrush BRN-Z looks similar to the Painbrush, however it has dark grey bristles and a bronze composition with traces of characteristic green copper patina . Whitish text is stamped under the joint of the swiveling brush head in a hyphenated format, possibly resembling a serial number. A packet resembling a field ration packet stamped with Barazushi's logo is attached to the side of the weapon, and the small ring separating the handle from the rest of the weapon is a dark green. This small ring never changes color to match the user's ink, unlike the original Painbrush.

## Trivia

The original tweet announcing this weapon accidentally refers to it as the Paintbrush BRN-Z.

The Painbrush BRN-Z shares its rusty design and its Barazushi field ration with other weapons in the Splatlands Collection : the Tri-Slosher ASH-N and the Splatana Wiper RUS-T .

## Etymology

The suffix "BRN-Z" is a stylized spelling of the word bronze , referencing the weapon's design.
