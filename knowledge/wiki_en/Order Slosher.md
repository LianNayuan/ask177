# Order Slosher

The Order Slosher is a slosher in Splatoon 3 that is exclusively used in Side Order .

## Appearance

The Order Slosher is based on the Slosher , but has a minimalist appearance, with a white molded bucket for the body of the weapon, as well as metallic silver handles and supports. When firing, ring-shaped holograms manifest around the opening at the top of the slosher.

## Side Order

The Order Slosher is a weapon unlocked in Side Order . Its base set consists of Suction Bombs and Triple Inkstrike . However, this can be changed in the Spire of Order by using a vending machine .

It has lower stats in comparison to the original Slosher , with a slower fire rate, damage, strafe speed, ink efficiency, ink coverage, and range. However, this can be upgraded through color chips and Marina's Hacks .

Big Man's Palette is one of the twelve Palettes in Side Order . It can be found in one of the lockers in front of the Spire of Order and is set to the Order Slosher. Defeating Overlorder with this Palette for the first time unlocks the Order Slosher Replica for use in online battles.
