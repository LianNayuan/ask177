# Tri-Slosher ASH-N

The Tri-Slosher ASH-N is a main weapon in the Splatoon series . It is a Barazushi -branded variant of the the Tri-Slosher from the Splatlands Collection and features a different sub and special weapon .

## Splatoon 3

- **Class**: Slosher
- **Sub**: Splat Bomb
- **Special**: Splattercolor Screen
- **Special points**: 180p
- **Range**: 31/100
- **Damage**: 75/100
- **Handling**: 70/100
- **Level**: 19
- **Added in**: Splatlands Collection ( 10.0.0 )
- **Other variants**: Tri-Slosher Tri-Slosher Nouveau

## Appearance

The Tri-Slosher ASH-N is similar to the Tri-Slosher, with the main difference being the bucket is now a dark gray instead of blue. The handle is now a dark brown and a packet resembling a field ration packet stamped with Barazushi's logo is attached to the right side.

## Etymology

The brand stylization "ASH-N" could be interpreted as ashen , regarding the weapon's coloration, or ashing .
