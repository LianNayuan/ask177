## Appearance

The Rapid Blaster Deco is nearly identical in appearance to the Rapid Blaster . The only difference between the two is that the Rapid Blaster Deco has stickers bearing the Deco logo on the sides.

In Splatoon 3 , the pink color that was present on the Rapid Blaster has been changed to blue.

## Trivia

The Rapid Blaster Deco is listed in the "Coming Soon" section of the Splatoon Prima Guide, even though it was available at launch. This could mean that it was originally intended to be a DLC item.

In Splatoon 3 , the Rapid Blaster have lights which illuminate when fired. But for some reason, these light-up parts on the Rapid Blaster Deco do not light up by any means. This issue is shared with the Rapid Blaster Pro Deco .
