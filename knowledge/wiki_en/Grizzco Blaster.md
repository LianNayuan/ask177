# Grizzco Blaster

The Grizzco Blaster is a Salmon Run rare weapon in Splatoon 2 and Splatoon 3 . It appears to be an illegitimately modified Blaster .

## Splatoon 3

- **Class**: Blaster
- **Base damage**: 35 (Splash) 50 (Direct hit)
- **Ink consumption**: 3%

## Splatoon 2

- **Class**: Shooter
- **Base damage**: 35 (Min. splash) 50 (Direct hit)
- **Ink consumption**: 3%

## Description

The weapon is a short-ranged blaster with an extremely high firing speed, possibly due to most of its frame being stripped down and modified. The range of the weapon is comparable to the Luna Blaster while the firing rate of the weapon is much greater than even the Clash Blaster .

## Appearance

The Grizzco Blaster is somewhat similar in appearance to the Blaster . However, the front has been completely stripped off, revealing the metal frame and inner workings. Multiple red and orange wires and metal blocks can be seen. Sticking out of the top are four yellow pill bottles with white caps, the contents of which are unknown. The ink tank has a dark green Grizzco Industries logo on the side.

## Details

The Grizzco Blaster has the same firing rate as the .52 Gal , shooting once every 3/20ths of a second.
