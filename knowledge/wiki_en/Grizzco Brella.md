# Grizzco Brella

The Grizzco Brella is a Salmon Run and Salmon Run Next Wave rare weapon in Splatoon 2 and Splatoon 3 . It appears to be an illegitimately modified Splat Brella .

## Splatoon 3

- **Class**: Brella
- **Base damage**: 15 per pellet (Cap: 80)
- **Ink consumption**: 2%

## Splatoon 2

- **Class**: Brella
- **Base damage**: 60
- **Ink consumption**: 2%

## Appearance

The Grizzco Brella is somewhat similar in appearance to the Splat Brella . The shield has been removed, revealing the metal poles usually covered by it. A small green and white lifesaver is strapped around the muzzle. Three yellow pill bottles with white caps and unknown contents are sticking out of the center pole.

## Trivia

Despite not having a canopy, the files for the Grizzco Brella do contain equivalent models to support having one, like other Brella weapons. Both of these models are shaped as small cubes, hidden inside the shaft of the brella, and are never visible or active during regular gameplay. These models are necessary for the weapon to function, even though they are never actually used. The game crashes as soon as the weapon is loaded, if they are to ever be removed.
