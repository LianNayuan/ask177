# Clash Blaster

The Clash Blaster is a main weapon in Splatoon 2 and Splatoon 3 , manufactured by Neo . It is a blaster with a high rate of fire and large blast radius, but with low damage.

## Description

The Clash Blaster fires at a short range, and at very high speed, but takes two direct hits to splat an opponent; from afar it takes around four hits. The Clash Blaster behaves as a pressure-based weapon. Compared to other blasters, which require a direct hit for quick splats, the Clash Blaster fires more rapidly, increasing mobility and turf coverage.

## Appearance

The Clash Blaster's main body is a light navy blue and shaped a bit like a pencil sharpener. Surrounding it is a ring of crayons that change color to match the user's ink that resembles a gun belt . Coming down from the body is a metal trigger and a handhold with black padding. Sticking out of the front of the body is a flat, rectangular, metal part that delivers the shots. Atop this part is a sight the same color as the body.

## Trivia

The Clash Blaster's design is likely based on a mechanical pencil sharpener, hence the crayons.

In Splatoon 2 , the Clash Blaster is one of the only three weapons to be unlocked simultaneously with its alternate kit, the Clash Blaster Neo . The other two are the H-3 Nozzlenose D and Cherry H-3 Nozzlenose , and the E-liter 4K Scope and Custom E-liter 4K Scope . It however remains as the only completely unique weapon. This is due to the Clash Blaster being unlocked at Level 30, which is the highest level for unlocking weapons.
