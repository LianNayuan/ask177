# Twinklez Splat Dualies

The Twinklez Splat Dualies is a main weapon in Splatoon 3 . It is an Emberz -branded version of the Splat Dualies .

## Splatoon 3

- **Class**: Dualie
- **Sub**: Fizzy Bomb
- **Special**: Big Bubbler
- **Special points**: 190p
- **Range**: 50/100
- **Damage**: 29/100
- **Mobility**: 60/100
- **Level**: 15
- **Added in**: Splatlands Collection ( 10.0.0 )
- **Other variants**: Splat Dualies Enperry Splat Dualies Order Dualie Replicas

## Appearance

The Twinklez Splat Dualies is similar to the Splat Dualies but with a new color scheme. The sections that were black are now a hot pink and the sections that were green are now a pale gray. The ink tanks are now silver with an Emberz badge on the side.

## Etymology

The word twinklez is a stylized spelling of the word twinkles .
