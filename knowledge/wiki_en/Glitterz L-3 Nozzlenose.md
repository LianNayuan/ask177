# Glitterz L-3 Nozzlenose

The Glitterz L-3 Nozzlenose is a main weapon in Splatoon 3 . It is the Emberz -branded version of the L-3 Nozzlenose and is part of the Splatlands Collection .

## Splatoon 3

- **Class**: Shooter
- **Sub**: Splat Bomb
- **Special**: Inkjet
- **Special points**: 200p
- **Range**: 62/100
- **Damage**: 30/100
- **Fire rate**: 65/100
- **Level**: 20
- **Added in**: Splatlands Collection ( 10.0.0 )
- **Base damage**: 31
- **Ink consumption**: 1.1% (3.3% per )
- **Other variants**: L-3 Nozzlenose L-3 Nozzlenose D

## Appearance

The Glitterz L-3 Nozzlenose has a similar apperence to the L-3 Nozzlenose but with a noticeable color change. The barrel, trigger, and coil discs of the hose are now a bright green, while the surrounding structure is now made of translucent pale green acrylic or resin inlaid with black glitter. The barrel also features new black text written in Inkling language, which is the same as that found on the Gleamz Blaster . The hose takes on a slight magenta tint. The hammer on the back of the weapon and the grip are now metallic gray. The sight is white, and two parts at the top front and bottom rear of the barrel are now orange. A small, gray, squid-shaped toy resembling a Tamagotchi is attached to the right side of the hose, and each of the three buttons below the toy is a color matching the Emberz logo's color scheme (neon red, neon green, and neon blue). The toy's small screen displays the brand's logo, while the Emberz text logo is engraved on the top of the toy.

## Etymology

The word glitterz is a stylized spelling of the word glitters .
