# Z+F Splatterscope

It is a rebranded successor of Firefin Splatterscope from Splatoon 2 following the merger between Firefin and Zekko to form Z+F.

## Splatoon 3

- **Class**: Charger
- **Sub**: Splash Wall
- **Special**: Triple Inkstrike
- **Special points**: 200p
- **Range**: 91/100
- **Charge speed**: 50/100
- **Mobility**: 40/100
- **Level**: 15
- **Added in**: Fresh Season 2023 ( 3.0.0 )
- **Other variants**: Splatterscope Splatterscope CAM-O

## Appearance

The Z+F Splatterscope is nearly identical to the Z+F Splat Charger . The only differences are that the sights that are closer to the user are removed, with the sights at the end of the barrel being maintained, and it has an added scope. The scope is identical to the scope on the Splatterscope in visual shape, but the middle is the same orange as used in much of the rest of the weapon and either end are the same purple as in the Z+F logo.
