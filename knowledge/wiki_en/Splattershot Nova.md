# Splattershot Nova

The Splattershot Nova is a main weapon in Splatoon 3 , manufactured by Splat . It is a long-range shooter that prioritizes rate of fire over damage.

## Splatoon 3

- **Class**: Shooter
- **Sub**: Point Sensor
- **Special**: Killer Wail 5.1
- **Special points**: 190p
- **Range**: 68/100
- **Damage**: 10/100
- **Fire rate**: 60/100
- **Level**: 8
- **Added in**: Chill Season 2022 ( 2.0.0 )
- **Base damage**: 24 30 ( SRNW )
- **Ink consumption**: 0.96%
- **Other variant**: Annaki Splattershot Nova

## Description

The Splattershot Nova has long range comparable to the Splattershot Pro but with a faster fire rate, less accuracy, and weaker damage, making its stats similar to the former Dual Squelcher from Splatoon . It is tied with the Aerospray series of weapons for the lowest damage stat of any shooter. The rate of fire is the same as that of the standard Splattershot .

## Appearance

The Splattershot Nova has a more "futuristic" space-themed design than most other Splattershot-named weapons. It consists of an oversized plastic sports water bottle matching the user's ink color slotted into a black shooter frame, causing it to resemble a ray gun or Super Soaker .

## Trivia

The Splattershot Nova is not included in Return of the Mammalians due to it being added post-release.
