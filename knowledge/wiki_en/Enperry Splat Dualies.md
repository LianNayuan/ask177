# Enperry Splat Dualies

The Enperry Splat Dualies are a main weapon in Splatoon 2 & Splatoon 3 . They are a variant of the Splat Dualies with the same stats but a different loadout and look, inspired by the gear brand Enperry .

## Splatoon 3

- **Class**: Dualie
- **Sub**: Curling Bomb
- **Special**: Triple Splashdown
- **Special points**: 190p
- **Range**: 50/100
- **Damage**: 29/100
- **Mobility**: 60/100
- **Level**: 10
- **Added in**: Chill Season 2023 ( 6.0.0 )
- **Other variants**: Splat Dualies Twinklez Splat Dualies Order Dualie Replicas

## Splatoon 2

- **Class**: Dualie
- **Sub**: Curling Bomb
- **Special**: Inkjet
- **Special points**: 200p
- **Range**: 50/100
- **Damage**: 29/100
- **Mobility**: 70/100
- **Level**: 11
- **Cost**: 9,000
- **Added in**: Initial Release ( 1.0.0 )
- **Base damage**: 30
- **Ink consumption**: 0.72% 7% (Roll)
- **Other variants**: Splat Dualies Kensa Splat Dualies Hero Dualie Replicas

## Appearance

The Enperry Splat Dualies share their builds with the Splat Dualies . However, the Enperry Splat Dualies are black and gold with the Enperry logo on the sides and highlights both in white.

## Trivia

In Splatoon 2 , the Enperry Splat Dualies share the same sub and special weapon as the Splat Dualies that appeared during the Splatoon 2 Global Testfire .

When doing the Triple Splashdown trial in Judd's trials , the player is given the Enperry Splat Dualies.
