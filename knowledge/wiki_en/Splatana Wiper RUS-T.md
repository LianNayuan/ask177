# Splatana Wiper RUS-T

The Splatana Wiper RUS-T is a main weapon in the Splatoon series . It is a Barazushi -branded variant of the the Splatana Wiper from the Splatlands Collection and features a different sub and special weapon .

## Splatoon 3

- **Class**: Splatana
- **Sub**: Curling Bomb
- **Special**: Trizooka
- **Special points**: 190p
- **Range**: 58/100
- **Damage**: 29/100
- **Handling**: 75/100
- **Level**: 14
- **Added in**: Splatlands Collection ( 10.0.0 )
- **Other variants**: Splatana Wiper Splatana Wiper Deco

## Appearance

The Splatana Wiper RUS-T is similar to the appearance of the Splatana Wiper , albeit with its handle and frame painted brown to resemble a rusty "finish". The handle has a packet resembling a field ration packet stamped with Barazushi's logo attached to it. The frame has white painted text which seems to be mimicking geographical coordinates written in the Serif number script , but whose meaning is unknown. Traces of oxidized green paint are visible on various parts of the weapon.

## Etymology

The brand stylization suffix "RUS-T" is derived from rust (or rusty , if the letter T is pronounced separately as its hyphenation could suggest), referring to the weapon's ostensibly rusted appearance.
