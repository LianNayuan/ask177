# Splattershot Pro FRZ-N

The Splattershot Pro FRZ-N is a main weapon in the Splatoon series . It is a Barazushi -branded variant of the the Splattershot Pro from the Splatlands Collection and features a different sub and special weapon .

## Splatoon 3

- **Class**: Shooter
- **Sub**: Splat Bomb
- **Special**: Tenta Missiles
- **Special points**: 190p
- **Range**: 70/100
- **Damage**: 64/100
- **Fire rate**: 30/100
- **Level**: 23
- **Added in**: Splatlands Collection ( 10.0.0 )
- **Base damage**: 45
- **Other variants**: Splattershot Pro Forge Splattershot Pro

## Appearance

The Splattershot Pro FRZ-N shares its overall design with Splattershot Pro . However, the part of the barrel that change color with ink color is instead chrome regardless of ink color, the grey parts of the mechanism over the top of the barrel are instead tan, the text on the barrel is replaced with the Barazushi logo, the red tube is changed to chrome with a red coral-shaped piece of fabric added, the remaining red is replaced with orange, and the blue is changed to dark sea green.
