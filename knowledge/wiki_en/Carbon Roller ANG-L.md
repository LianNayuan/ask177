# Carbon Roller ANG-L

The Carbon Roller ANG-L is a main weapon in the Splatoon series . It is a Barazushi -branded variant of the the Carbon Roller from the Splatlands Collection and features a different sub and special weapon .

## Splatoon 3

- **Class**: Roller
- **Sub**: Fizzy Bomb
- **Special**: Super Chump
- **Special points**: 170p
- **Range**: 20/100
- **Ink speed**: 63/100
- **Handling**: 65/100
- **Level**: 23
- **Added in**: Splatlands Collection ( 10.0.0 )
- **Other variants**: Carbon Roller Carbon Roller Deco

## Appearance

The Carbon Roller ANG-L shares its overall design with the Splatoon 3 Carbon Roller . However, the tank is a chrome that doesn't change with ink color, the bottle holder is changed to orange, the yellow-orange parts of its frame are now a dark and somewhat see-through color with a yellow-orange internal tube visible inside, a light is attached to the left side of the frame, the previously black parts are a slightly darker shade of black, the text on one half of the black disk on the end of the roller is replaced by the Barazushi logo with the triangle, the half of the circles on the outer edges of the rolling surface that are on the side of the user by default are a light shade of grey, and the other side of the circles on the outer edges of the rolling surface are a off-green.

## Etymology

The brand stylization suffix "ANG-L" is derived from anglerfish .
