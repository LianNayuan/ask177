# Aerospray MG

The Aerospray MG has an extremely high fire rate, but each shot is weak individually. It takes five hits to splat an opponent. It shares its spot as the shooter with the lowest damage with the Splattershot Nova . It shoots with high spread, allowing its users to quickly cover turf and to hit opponents without perfectly aiming at them.

## Appearance

The Aerospray MG is a short, silver gun reminiscent of an aerosol spray pen, with a large tank on top bearing the Ammo Knights logo in black. Underneath the tank is a silver pipe that serves as a barrel with a wide nozzle on the end. Attaching the tank to the barrel is a pair of silver supports. Underneath the nozzle is a metal slope seemingly designed to stop ink from getting on the user's hand. In the back of the Aerospray MG is a thin black tube connecting the side of the barrel to the bottom.

## Trivia

The Aerospray MG appears to be designed after an aerograph, a type of airbrush.

The MG in this weapon's name likely stands for "Master Grade", referencing Bandai's line of 1:100 scale model kits for the anime Mobile Suit Gundam. This is likely because it is common for model kits to be painted with an airbrush and Gundam models are extremely popular in Japan.

In Splatoon 2 , the Aerospray MG was the only weapon to be unlocked at its level for more than a year-from its debut in July 2017 to the Kensa Splattershot 's debut in September 2018.

This is one of the weapons available to use in the Splatoon 3 Splatfest World Premiere .

When Fizzy Bomb or Reefslider come up in Judd's trials , the player is equipped with the Aerospray MG in both occasions.
