## Appearance

The Rapid Blaster Pro Deco is nearly identical in appearance to the Rapid Blaster Pro . The only difference between the two is that the Rapid Blaster Pro Deco has stickers bearing the Deco logo on the sides. In Splatoon 3 , the detailing on the weapon body is also blue instead of red.

## Trivia

In Splatoon 3 , the lights on the sides of the Rapid Blaster Pro Deco does not illuminate when fired, but the others versions of the weapon does. This issue can also be seen on the Rapid Blaster Deco .
