# Octo Shot

The Octo Shot , also formatted as Octoshot in Splatoon , is a shooter in the Splatoon series . It is used by enemy Octolings in Octo Valley , Octo Canyon , Octo Expansion , and Return of the Mammalians . Additionally, it is used by Agent 8 during the escape phases of the Octo Expansion and when setting Eight's Palette to the Pearl Drone in Side Order .

## Splatoon 3

- **Class**: Shooter
- **Sub**: Splat Bomb
- **Special**: Triple Splashdown
- **Base damage**: 240

## Splatoon 2

- **Class**: Shooter
- **Base damage**: 35
- **Ink consumption**: 1%

## Appearance

The Octo Shot's overall appearance is similar to that of the original Splattershot from Splatoon , albeit with an overall aesthetic more closely resembling that of a firearm than a toy. The Octo Shot's ink tank is a large cylindrical tank with a black screw cap which rests on top of the main parts of the gun with a thick, plastic, black band wrapped around it, connecting the ink tank to the gun. From the top of the screw cap, a large wide tube bends in a U-turn fashion connecting the ink to the barrel of the Octo Shot, which resembles a pure black and miniature version of the ink tank, complete with a black screw cap. Below the barrel is a large pistol grip with a small trigger that is held within one hand. Connecting on the remaining side of the barrel is a foregrip, held with the other hand to stabilize the weapon when firing. The foregrip is a silver-colored semi-cylinder with a black semi-cylinder coating the outside, complete with finger holds for grip. Connecting to the forward-facing side of the foregrip is a funnel-shaped silver nozzle. The default appearance and Octo Shot thumbnails portray the ink color to be a dark purple.

## Trivia

In Splatoon , the Octo Shot is the only weapon to appear but not be usable by the player in any way.

The Octo Shot is not tied to a specific weapon set; although enemy Octolings in Octo Valley and Octo Canyon only use it with Splat Bombs , those in the Octo Expansion and Return of the Mammalians can use various different sub and special weapons with it.

The Octo Shot bears a resemblance to the IMI Uzi submachine gun.

The Octo Shot is the only weapon in the Octo Expansion that Agent 8 can start a level with but can never obtain from an equipper , as the one used in the escape phases is instead a canned weapon .

Although it is intended for the player to be forced to collect the Octo Shot in the Villi Phase , it is possible to avoid it by using an exploit . However, when the player moves on to the Belly Phase , they will be equipped with the Octo Shot for the rest of the escape phases like usual, regardless of whether they actually collected it.
