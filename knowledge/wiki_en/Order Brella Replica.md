# Order Brella Replica

The Order Brella Replica is a main weapon in Splatoon 3 . It is a reskin of the Splat Brella , and a direct successor to the Splatoon 2 Hero Brella Replica .

## Splatoon 3

- **Class**: Brella
- **Sub**: Sprinkler
- **Special**: Triple Inkstrike
- **Special points**: 210p
- **Range**: 43/100
- **Damage**: 65/100
- **Durability**: 60/100
- **Level**: 0
- **Added in**: Side Order ( 7.0.0 )
- **Base damage**: 16.2 per pellet (Cap: 81) 30 (Contact)
- **Other variants**: Splat Brella Sorella Brella

## Appearance

The Order Brella Replica is designed to look like a simplified version of the Splat Brella , containing only white and gray colors.
