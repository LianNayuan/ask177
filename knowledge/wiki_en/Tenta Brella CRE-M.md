# Tenta Brella CRE-M

The Tenta Brella CRE-M is a main weapon in the Splatoon series . It is a Barazushi -branded variant of the the Tenta Brella from the Splatlands Collection and features a different sub and special weapon .

## Splatoon 3

- **Class**: Brella
- **Sub**: Toxic Mist
- **Special**: Super Chump
- **Special points**: 180p
- **Range**: 62/100
- **Damage**: 85/100
- **Durability**: 85/100
- **Level**: 25
- **Added in**: Splatlands Collection ( 10.0.0 )
- **Other variants**: Tenta Brella Tenta Sorella Brella

## Appearance

The Tenta Brella CRE-M shares its overall design with the Tenta Brella . However, it has a more plain style, being colored mainly in gray and beige. Two black Barazushi logos are on either side of the muzzle on the canopy, and two orange straps with the Barazushi branding are attached with karabiners to the bars. Unlike the Tenta Brella, no part of the canopy change color to match the user's ink.

## Etymology

The suffix "CRE-M" is a stylized spelling of the word cream .
