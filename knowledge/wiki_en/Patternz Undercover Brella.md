# Patternz Undercover Brella

The Patternz Undercover Brella is a main weapon in Splatoon 3 . It is a Emberz -branded version of the Undercover Brella and comes with a different sub weapon and special weapon .

## Splatoon 3

- **Class**: Brella
- **Sub**: Curling Bomb
- **Special**: Killer Wail 5.1
- **Special points**: 180p
- **Range**: 50/100
- **Damage**: 30/100
- **Durability**: 25/100
- **Level**: 27
- **Added in**: Splatlands Collection ( 10.0.0 )
- **Other variants**: Undercover Brella Undercover Sorella Brella

## Appearance

The Patternz Undercover Brella shares its overall design with the Undercover Brella . However, the Patternz Undercover Brella has a white handle and muzzle, and the shield has different patterns on it instead of being pure black. The canopy is divided in eight sections, following the ribs: four sections have a diagonal lines pattern with half of them yellow and the other half purple, and the rest of the sections are filled with different holographic stickers. The Emberz logo and branding can be seen on one of them.

## Etymology

The word patternz is a stylized spelling of the word patterns .
