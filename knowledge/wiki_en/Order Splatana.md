# Order Splatana

The Order Splatana is a splatana in Splatoon 3 that is exclusively used in Side Order .

## Appearance

The Order Splatana is based on the Splatana Stamper , but has a minimalist appearance, with a white molded chain and base for the body of the weapon, as well as a metallic silver handle at the bottom. When used, ring-shaped holograms manifest around the lower base of the weapon.

## Side Order

The Order Splatana is a weapon unlocked in Side Order . Its base set consists of Fizzy Bombs and Crab Tank . However, this can be changed in the Spire of Order by using a vending machine .

It has lower stats in comparison to the Splatana Stamper, with a slower swing rate, strafe speed, ink efficiency, ink coverage, range, and charge time, but has higher projectile damage. However, this can be upgraded through color chips and Marina's Hacks .

Frye's Palette is one of the twelve Palettes in Side Order . It can be found in one of the lockers in front of the Spire of Order and is set to the Order Splatana. Defeating Overlorder with this Palette for the first time unlocks the Order Splatana Replica for use in online battles.
