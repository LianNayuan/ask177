# Planetz Big Swig Roller

The Planetz Big Swig Roller is a main weapon in Splatoon 3 , and is the Emberz -branded version of the Big Swig Roller . It was added as part of the Splatlands Collection on 12 June 2025 .

## Splatoon 3

- **Class**: Roller
- **Sub**: Torpedo
- **Special**: Triple Splashdown
- **Special points**: 180p
- **Range**: 56/100
- **Ink speed**: 54/100
- **Handling**: 60/100
- **Level**: 21
- **Added in**: Splatlands Collection ( 10.0.0 )
- **Other variants**: Big Swig Roller Big Swig Roller Express

## Appearance

The Planetz Big Swig Roller has a similar appereance to the Big Swig Roller with a different color scheme. The sections that were blue are now a light red and the sections above the cup that was light blue is purple. The handle is also now a light gray. The cup's design was changed, featuring more Emberz stickers. The Emberz badge is found on the cup.

## Etymology

The word planetz is a stylized spelling of the word planets .
