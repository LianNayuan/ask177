# Custom Hydra Splatling

The Hydra Splatling must be charged up to fire a continuous spray of ink, like all splatlings . It distinguishes itself from the others by having the longest range and slowest charge time. It is the only splatling capable of splatting opponents with three ink bullets, as it has an exclusive high-damage feature that activates when the weapon is fully charged. The Hydra Splatling's mobility is low, so speed is impaired when it is equipped, even when not attacking with the weapon.

## Appearance

The Custom Hydra Splatling is nearly identical in appearance to the Hydra Splatling . The only two differences between the two is that the Custom Hydra Splatling has a sticker bearing the Custom logo on the side of the tank, and, in Splatoon 3 , the addition of the hose that connects the tank to the weapon now being light blue instead of yellow.

## Trivia

In Splatoon , at 29,900 , the Custom Hydra Splatling is the most expensive splatling and the second most expensive weapon in the game.

After the Custom Hydra Splatling's release, Nintendo had stated that it would be the last downloadable weapon coming to Splatoon . However, on 3 March 2016 Nintendo Direct presentation contradicted this. It showed at least eight new weapons that would be released.

At 33,300 , the Custom Hydra Splatling is the most expensive weapon in Splatoon 2 .

While this weapon does have enough power to take down a player using a Booyah Bomb 's armour in a single full charge, the timing is so precise the player may have thrown their bomb by the time the armor breaks. Main Power Up and Object Shredder can help with this.

In its Splatoon 3 reveal post by @SplatoonNA on Twitter, the weapon was mistakenly marked as having Point Sensor . The tweet was later deleted, and a corrected one was posted.

## Etymology

Custom Hydra Splatling is derived from Custom ; Hydra , a mythological multi-headed water monster, and a portmanteau of Splat and Gatling . Hydra may also be short for Hydrant , since the Custom Hydra Splatling is based on a fire hydrant in design. The prefix Custom is also used to differentiate it from the Hydra Splatling . This trait is shared among the other alternate sets of the Custom brand.

ハイドラントカスタム Haidorantokasutamu is derived from Hydrant and Custom . Hydrant refers to a fire hydrant. Like the English name, "Custom" is also used to differentiate it from Hydrant.
