# Custom E-liter 4K

The Custom E-liter 4K is a main weapon in Splatoon 2 and Splatoon 3 . It is the Custom version of the E-liter 4K .

## Splatoon 3

- **Class**: Charger
- **Sub**: Squid Beakon
- **Special**: Kraken Royale
- **Special points**: 210p
- **Range**: 96/100
- **Charge speed**: 20/100
- **Mobility**: 15/100
- **Level**: 20
- **Added in**: Fresh Season 2024
- **Base damage**: 40–80 (Partial charge) 180 (Full charge)
- **Other variant**: E-liter 4K

## Splatoon 2

- **Class**: Charger
- **Sub**: Squid Beakon
- **Special**: Bubble Blower
- **Special points**: 190p
- **Range**: 96/100
- **Charge speed**: 20/100
- **Mobility**: 15/100
- **Level**: 26
- **Cost**: 17,300
- **Added in**: 14 October 2017
- **Base damage**: 40–80 (Partial charge) 180 (Full charge)
- **Ink consumption**: 2.25% (No charge) 25% (Full charge)
- **Other variant**: E-liter 4K

## Appearance

In Splatoon 2 , the Custom E-liter 4K is nearly identical in appearance to the E-liter 4K . The only difference between the two is that the Custom E-liter 4K has a sticker bearing the Custom logo on the ink tank.

In Splatoon 3 , the Custom sticker is still present, but the color of the weapon's plastic grips/supports is now red instead of blue.
