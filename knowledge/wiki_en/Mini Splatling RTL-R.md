# Mini Splatling RTL-R

The Mini Splatling RTL-R is a main weapon in the Splatoon series . It is a Barazushi -branded variant of the Mini Splatling , with identical stats.

## Splatoon 3

- **Class**: Splatling
- **Sub**: Squid Beakon
- **Special**: Trizooka
- **Special points**: 200p
- **Range**: 62/100
- **Charge speed**: 80/100
- **Mobility**: 90/100
- **Level**: 27
- **Added in**: Splatlands Collection ( 10.0.0 )
- **Other variants**: Mini Splatling Zink Mini Splatling

## Appearance

Compared to the Mini Splatling , the Mini Splatling RTL-R has a pale green color with a snake-like pattern instead of the usual blue, and a light brown color instead of yellow. The tank cap, the trigger and the six barrel tips are orange, while the reservoir and the hose connected to it are metallic. No part of the weapon changes color to match the user's ink . A decoration resembling a leather strap with a spiked metal tool is attached to the front of the weapon.

## Etymology

The suffix "RTL-R" is a stylized spelling of the word rattler , a common name for rattlesnakes , regarding the weapon's snake skin-like texture.
