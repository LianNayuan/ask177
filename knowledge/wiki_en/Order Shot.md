# Order Shot

The Order Shot is a shooter in Splatoon 3 that is exclusively used in Side Order .

## Appearance

The Order Shot is based on the Splattershot , but has a minimalist appearance, with a white molded piece for the barrel, ink tank, and handle of the weapon, as well as a metallic silver end cap and nozzle. When firing, ring-shaped holograms manifest around the nozzle of the Shooter.

## Side Order

The Order Shot is a weapon unlocked in Side Order . Its base set consists of Splat Bombs and Trizooka . However, this can be changed in the Spire of Order by using a vending machine .

It has lower stats in comparison to the Splattershot , with a slower fire rate, damage, strafe speed, ink efficiency, and range. However, this can be upgraded through color chips and Marina's Hacks .

Agent 4's Palette is the third Palette in Side Order . It is available after completing the first climb of the Spire of Order and is set to the Order Shot. The Palette is found in the third locker , which can be opened with the keys given by Marina after the player approached the spire and talked to her. Defeating Overlorder with this Palette for the first time unlocks the Order Shot Replica for use in online battles.
