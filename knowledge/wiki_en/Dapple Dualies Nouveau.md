# Dapple Dualies Nouveau

The Dapple Dualies Nouveau are a main weapon in Splatoon 2 and Splatoon 3 . They are the Nouveau branded version of the Dapple Dualies .

- **Class**: Dualie
- **Sub**: Torpedo
- **Special**: Reefslider
- **Special points**: 190p
- **Range**: 24/100
- **Damage**: 47/100
- **Mobility**: 80/100
- **Level**: 26
- **Added in**: Chill Season 2022 ( 2.0.0 )
- **Other variants**: Dapple Dualies Dapple Dualies NOC-T

## Appearance

The Dapple Dualies Nouveau share their builds with the Dapple Dualies . However, the Dapple Dualies Nouveau are mainly dark blue and have a sticker bearing the Nouveau logo on the top of each dualie.

## Trivia

There are toothbrushes on the ends of the guns, likely referencing the fact that toothbrushes are used to make realistic splatter marks known as "dapples" on paintings.

The sticker used for the Dapple Dualies Nouveau's icon is different in the games it appears in. In Splatoon 2 , the sticker used for the icon is unique, while in Splatoon 3 , it uses the same sticker as the other Nouveau-branded weapons.

Unlike most weapons in the game, whose locker stickers are the same size between the different kit variations, the Dapple Dualies Nouveau locker sticker is a different size from the locker sticker for the Dapple Dualies . It is notably larger than the unbranded variant's sticker.
