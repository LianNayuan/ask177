# Bloblobber Deco

The Bloblobber Deco is a main weapon in Splatoon 2 and Splatoon 3 . It is a Deco -branded variant of the Bloblobber .

## Splatoon 3

- **Class**: Slosher
- **Sub**: Angle Shooter
- **Special**: Kraken Royale
- **Special points**: 190p
- **Range**: 85/100
- **Damage**: 29/100
- **Handling**: 50/100
- **Level**: 23
- **Added in**: Drizzle Season 2023 ( 5.0.0 )
- **Base damage**: 32
- **Other variant**: Bloblobber

## Splatoon 2

- **Class**: Slosher
- **Sub**: Sprinkler
- **Special**: Suction-Bomb Launcher
- **Special points**: 190p
- **Range**: 85/100
- **Damage**: 29/100
- **Handling**: 50/100
- **Level**: 20
- **Cost**: 17,600
- **Added in**: 4.3.0
- **Base damage**: 30
- **Ink consumption**: 8%
- **Other variant**: Bloblobber

## Appearance

The Bloblobber Deco is nearly identical in appearance to the Bloblobber .

In Splatoon 2 , the Bloblobber Deco has some stickers bearing the Deco logo on the supports.

In Splatoon 3 , the supports are colored yellow and the stickers are colored navy blue.

## Trivia

Early datamining in Splatoon 2 showed the Bloblobber Deco was going to have the Fizzy Bomb , but this was later changed to the Sprinkler .
