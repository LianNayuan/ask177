# Painbrush

The Painbrush is a main weapon in Splatoon 3 , manufactured by Nouveau . It is a brush with higher power and range than other weapons in its class.

## Splatoon 3

- **Class**: Brush
- **Sub**: Curling Bomb
- **Special**: Wave Breaker
- **Special points**: 190p
- **Range**: 33/100
- **Ink speed**: 85/100
- **Handling**: 60/100
- **Level**: 14
- **Added in**: Sizzle Season 2023 ( 4.0.0 )
- **Base damage**: 31-60 (Splash) 30 (Roll) 75-150 ( SRNW Splash) 50 ( SRNW Roll)
- **Ink consumption**: 4.8% (Flinging ink) 10.8% per second (Sliding)
- **Other variants**: Painbrush Nouveau Painbrush BRN-Z

## Appearance

The Painbrush resembles a fan brush with a slit in its bristles. Its silver, metallic handle resembles a palette cup. On the handle near the bristles, the Nouveau logo can be seen, colored white. The ring separating the handle from the rest of the weapon changes color matching user's color. The handle is made of wood.

## Trivia

The Painbrush is not included in Return of the Mammalians due to being added post-release.

The Painbrush is the first new entry in the brush class since the original roster from Splatoon .

The Painbrush is the only brush since the original Splatoon to have the ability to splat an enemy in two hits.

Despite its metallic appearance similar to the Dynamo Roller , the Aerospray and the Nautilus , the Painbrush isn't an Ammo Knights weapon, but a Nouveau one.

## Etymology

Painbrush is a combination of the words pain , referencing the high damage of the weapon, and brush while also being a pun on paintbrush .
