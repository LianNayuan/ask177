## Appearance

The L-3 Nozzlenose D in Splatoon and Splatoon 2 is nearly identical in appearance to the L-3 Nozzlenose, with the only difference being the presence of its manufacturer's dolphin decal on the hose.

In Splatoon 3 , the L-3 Nozzlenose D has undergone a noticeable visual change, setting it further apart from its standard counterpart: its build is now significantly lighter in color, with most of the frame becoming white and all of the darker blue elements shifting to a significantly lighter shade. It retains the dolphin decal.

## Trivia

Across all three games, the L-3 Nozzlenose D has had Burst Bomb as its sub and various forms of an offense-based special.

## Etymology

The appended "D" in this weapon's name likely stands for "Dolphin", matching both the logo of its manufacturer and the dolphin theming of the base weapon's name in some languages.
